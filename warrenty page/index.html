Car Warranty Information

Warranty Overview

Our warranty is designed to give you peace of mind when purchasing a vehicle from us. It covers a wide range of components and provides excellent protection for both new and certified pre-owned vehicles. This warranty includes extensive repair coverage, parts replacement, and offers additional support through roadside assistance.

Warranty Terms and Coverage

Our standard warranty includes the following terms:

Component Coverage Period Conditions
Engine 5 years / 60,000 miles Includes all internal parts, cooling system, and seals
Transmission 5 years / 60,000 miles Automatic and manual transmissions, transfer cases
Electrical Systems 3 years / 36,000 miles Covers alternators, starters, wiring harnesses
Suspension 3 years / 36,000 miles Covers shocks, struts, and control arms
Roadside Assistance 2 years / unlimited miles 24/7 assistance with towing, battery jump-starts, and more

Warranty Exclusions

While our warranty is comprehensive, there are certain exclusions. Please note that the following are not covered under the warranty:

  • Normal wear and tear (e.g., brake pads, tires, wiper blades).
  • Damage due to accidents, misuse, or neglect.
  • Modifications not performed by authorized dealers or service centers.
  • Cosmetic issues (e.g., paint, upholstery, trim) after 12 months.
  • Environmental damage (e.g., flood, fire, hail).

How to File a Warranty Claim

Filing a warranty claim is straightforward. Follow these steps to ensure your claim is processed smoothly:

  • Contact our customer service at 1-800-123-4567 or email us at warranty@cardealer.com.
  • Provide your vehicle identification number (VIN) and a description of the issue.
  • Schedule an appointment with one of our authorized service centers.
  • Bring your vehicle to the service center for diagnosis and repair.
  • All parts and labor will be covered if the issue is within warranty terms.

Optional Extended Warranty

For customers who want additional peace of mind, we offer an extended warranty that can cover your vehicle for up to 10 years or 100,000 miles. This extended plan includes:

  • Expanded coverage for major components like the engine, transmission, and drivetrain.
  • Protection for high-tech systems, including infotainment and navigation.
  • Flexible payment plans for adding extended coverage to your vehicle.
  • Warranty transfers in case you sell your vehicle.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is covered under the powertrain warranty?

The powertrain warranty covers the engine, transmission, and drivetrain, including all internal lubricated parts. It also covers certain components of the transfer case, axles, and driveshaft.

Can I transfer my warranty to a new owner?

Yes, the warranty is transferable to the new owner if you decide to sell your vehicle. The new owner will benefit from the remaining coverage period.

What should I do if my vehicle breaks down outside of normal working hours?

You can contact our 24/7 roadside assistance team at 1-800-123-4567. We will arrange for towing and repairs as soon as possible.

Do I need to use authorized service centers?

Yes, to maintain your warranty coverage, all repairs must be performed by one of our authorized service centers. This ensures that only genuine parts are used and repairs meet manufacturer standards.
